# üåå COSMOS - Interactive Space Explorer

**COSMOS** is an immersive educational web application designed to bring the wonders of the solar system and astrophysics to your screen. Built with modern web technologies, it combines scientific data with stunning 3D visuals, real-time NASA integrations, and interactive physics simulations.

## üöÄ Features

### üî≠ Interactive 3D Visualizations
- **NASA Eyes Integration**: Explore high-fidelity, interactive 3D models of **Earth** and **Mars** directly within the application, powered by NASA's Eyes technology.
- **3D Solar System**: View rotating 3D models of all 8 planets with scientifically accurate ring systems (e.g., Saturn's complex rings vs. Uranus's faint rings).
- **Milky Way Galaxy**: A mesmerizing, rotating visualization of our home galaxy.
- **Interactive Controls**: Toggle between 2D schematic views and 3D models for detailed exploration.

### ‚öõÔ∏è Physics & Simulations
- **Cosmic Travel Calculator**: Plan interstellar journeys! Calculate travel distance (AU/km) and time between planets using:
  - *Speed of Light (c)*
  - *New Horizons Probe*
  - *Voyager 1*
  - *Future Ion Drives*
  - Includes special precise calculations for Earth-Moon travel.
- **Gravity Simulator**: Discover your weight on different worlds, from the crushing gravity of Jupiter to the lightness of the Moon.
- **Orbital Mechanics**: Interactive modules explaining Newton's and Kepler's Laws, orbits, and tidal forces.

### üìö Deep Dive Modules
- **Planet Details**: Comprehensive data on Physical Properties, Internal Structure, Atmosphere, and Magnetosphere for every planet.
- **Extreme Physics**: Explore mind-bending phenomena:
  - **Supermassive Black Holes**: A visually stunning simulation of a black hole with a rotating accretion disk and event horizon.
  - **Diamond Rain**: Learn about the exotic precipitation on Ice Giants.
  - **Metallic Hydrogen**: Discover the strange states of matter inside Gas Giants.
- **Habitability**: Understand the "Goldilocks Zone" and the search for extraterrestrial life.
- **Planetary Formation**: Learn about the Solar Nebula hypothesis, accretion, and differentiation.

### üì° Real-Time Data & Assets
- **Real Planet Images**: High-resolution imagery for all celestial bodies, replacing generic placeholders.
- **NASA APOD**: Live integration with NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day API to showcase the latest discoveries.
- **Stars & Galaxies**: Detailed cards on stellar types (Red Dwarfs, Supergiants) and our galactic neighborhood.

### üé® Modern UI/UX
- **Glassmorphism Design**: Sleek, translucent UI elements containing rich educational content.
- **Immersive Environment**: Dynamic, rotating starfields and galaxy backgrounds.
- **Smooth Animations**: Powered by **Framer Motion** for fluid page transitions and entry effects.
- **Responsive Design**: optimized for various screen sizes.
